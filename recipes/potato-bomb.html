Potato Bomb

- Description -

Its an simple, easy and fast recipe of potato in the airfryer, it tastes good but inst health at all, and is a bomb of calories

- Ingredients -

  • Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Sweet Paprika

- Steps -

  1. Wash and cut the potatoes in two, and make little cuts on the slices that you have without splitting it
  2. Put the olive oil on the potato with a brush tool
  3. Just put all the other ingredients(Salt, Black Pepper, Paprika, Sweet Paprika)
  4. Put the potatoes in the airfryer on 200 degrees for 30 minutes
